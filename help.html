Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. We'll also send you email/SMS updates with tracking information once your order is shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and with all accessories. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery (1-2 business days). Delivery times may vary based on your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders.
